# VeloHealth
VeloHealth: Symptom Drift TrackerLong-Term Disease Evolution AIVeloHealth is a longitudinal health intelligence engine designed to detect "Symptom Drift"—the subtle, 1% weekly declines in chronic conditions (like Parkinson’s or MS) that are invisible to the human eye but signify serious disease progression.🚀 The ProblemChronic diseases don't always "flare"; they often evolve slowly.The "New Normal" Trap: Patients adapt to slow declines and forget to report them.Snapshot Medicine: Doctors only see a 15-minute "snapshot" every few months, missing the trajectory of the disease.💡 The SolutionVeloHealth shifts the focus from State to Velocity. By correlating passive smartphone biometrics with active reports, it calculates a Drift Coefficient ($D$) to predict crises before they happen.Core Innovation: The "Digital Twin" BaselineUnlike apps that compare you to a "standard human," our AI uses Self-Supervised Temporal Contrastive Learning to compare you to your own 6-month historical baseline.✨ Key FeaturesPassive Micro-Signals: Silently tracks gait symmetry, typing cadence, and voice jitter via smartphone sensors.Multi-Modal Signal Fusion: Correlates biometrics with environmental metadata (weather/pressure) to filter out "noise."Predictive Triage: A "Traffic Light" dashboard for clinicians to prioritize high-velocity patients.Adaptive Feedback: Automated micro-surveys triggered only when the AI detects a negative trend.🛠️ Technical ArchitectureCore AI: Temporal Fusion Transformers (TFT) for time-series forecasting.Database: TimeScaleDB (PostgreSQL) for high-resolution telemetry.Standards: HL7 FHIR APIs for EMR integration.Processing: 14-day rolling Gaussian filters to smooth stochastic volatility and highlight true trends.🌍 Impact & BenefitsFor Patients: Validates "invisible" symptoms with objective data; inclusive design for those with poor vision.For Families: "Care Circle" dashboards provide peace of mind and early warnings.For Systems: Reduces ER visits by shifting from expensive emergency care to proactive intervention.Economic: Saves thousands per patient annually by extending "quality-of-life" years through precision medicine.🛠️ Installation & Setup(Placeholder for your specific repo instructions)Clone the repository.Install dependencies: pip install -r requirements.txtInitialize the Drift Engine: python main.py --mode train
